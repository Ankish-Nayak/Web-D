Basic document flow

I am a basic block level element. My adjeacnet block level elements sit on new lines below me.

By default we span 100% of the width of our parent elements, and we are as our child content. Our total width and height is our content + padding + border width/height.

We are separated by our margins. Because of martgin collapsing, we are separated by th width of one of our margins, not both.
